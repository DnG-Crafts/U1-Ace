## Feeder Covers

<br>


Ace Feeder - Left<br>
Ace Feeder - Right<br>
<br>

These covers allow you to add m10 connectors to the U1 feeders.
The connectors are required because the ACE will push the filament during loading and feed assist so a secure connection is needed.<br><br>
I printed mine using PETG, white for the cover, black for the numbers and yellow translucent for the led light covers.

<br><br>
Ace Feeder Blank<br>
<br>

This cover is used to cover the feeder holes and cables when you remove the U1 feeders for the direct ace connection to the U1.<br>
You can print these out of any material as they are just covers and have no structural requirement.
